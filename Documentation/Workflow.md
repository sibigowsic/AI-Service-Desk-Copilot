# Workflow

## AI Incident Lifecycle

```
User reports issue
        │
        ▼

Search Knowledge Base
        │

 ┌───────────────┐
 │               │

 ▼               ▼

Article Found   No Article

 │               │

 ▼               ▼

Provide Fix   Create Incident

                    │

                    ▼

          Retrieve Status

                    │

                    ▼

          Update Work Notes

                    │

                    ▼

            Close Incident
```

---

## Example Scenario

### User

> My laptop cannot connect to office WiFi.

↓

AI searches the Knowledge Base.

↓

No article resolves the issue.

↓

Creates a ServiceNow Incident.

↓

User later asks

> What's the status?

↓

AI retrieves incident details.

↓

Support engineer updates work notes.

↓

Issue resolved.

↓

AI closes the incident.

- Faster ticket creation
